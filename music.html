THE
SOUND
OF TRUST

Every song on Unconditional was made at night. Zeke has talked about this in interviews — the way darkness strips away performance, leaves only what's real.

The result is a record that sounds like 2am in someone else's apartment. Familiar. Intimate. Like being let inside something private.

That's not an accident. It's the whole architecture of what Zeke is building — music as a space you enter, not a show you watch.
